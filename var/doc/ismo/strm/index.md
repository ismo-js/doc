# Mappings
## `x` *join*
```js
> @[
>   @[x=> x+1],
>   @[1,2,3],
> ]::x()
@[2,3,4]

> @[
>   @[-,x=> x+1,x=> x+2],
>   @[1,-,-,2],
> ]::x()
@[-,2,3,4]

> @[
>   @[to],
>   @[5],
>   @[a,-,b,d],
> ]::x()
@[5,-,5,5]

> @[
>   @[filter],
>   @[x=> a === x],
>   @[a,-,b,d],
> ]::x()
@[a,-,-,-]

> @[
>   @[when],
>   @[-,-,a,-,-,b],
>   @[1],
> ]::x()
@[-,-,1,-,-,1]
```
